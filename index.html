About me

I built a UX practice from scratch.
Here's what I learned.

I'm a UX generalist embedded in a systems engineering environment at GTRI, where I've created and implemented a design practice in an organization that had none. I've had to earn every seat at every table — which has made me a better researcher and a more effective communicator.

My work spans workflow diagramming, wireframing, stakeholder research, and translating deeply technical systems into experiences that make sense to real people. I care about the craft and the impact in equal measure.
